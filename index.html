Oreo Desert

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 15
Ingredients Quantity
Oreos 2 Packages
Vanila Ice Cream One pint
Chocolare Syrup Optional
Buter 4 tablespoons

Step 1: Mash up all oreos
Step 2 : Mix Mashed up oreos with butter
Step 3 Put one layer of oreos on the bottem of your container
Step 4 Put a thick layer of Ice Cream over the oreos.
Step 5 Put a layer of chocolate syrup over the ice cream
Step 6 Repeat steps 3-5 untill your container is full.
